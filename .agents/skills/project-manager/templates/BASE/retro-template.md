## Retrospective Format Options

### Format A: 4Ls
- **Liked:** What the team liked
- **Learned:** What the team learned
- **Lacked:** What was missing
- **Longed For:** What the team wished for

### Format B: Start/Stop/Continue
- **Start:** New practices
- **Stop:** Things to quit
- **Continue:** What's working

### Format C: Mad/Sad/Glad
- **Mad:** Frustrations
- **Sad:** Disappointments
- **Glad:** Celebrations

### Format D: Sailboat
- **Wind:** What propelled us forward
- **Anchor:** What held us back
- **Rocks:** Risks we avoided
- **Island:** Our destination/goal
